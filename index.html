Welcome to Nicaragua

As my native country, Nicaragua was once a very beautiful vacation destination. I look back to photos from this trip and am grateful I got to enjoy its beauty before it became overrun by the corrupt government. I hope this vacation website offers you a different, more positive perspective on Nicaragua.

About Nicaragua

Must-Know Facts

  • Nicaragua is located in the heart of Central America.
  • Nickname: “The Land of Lakes and Volcanoes.”
  • Country name meaning: “Here united with the water.”
  • Main Language: Spanish
  • Its three regions vary greatly in weather, geography, and population.
  • Region names: Pacific, Central, and Caribbean.
  • Rhythmic marimbas and folkloric dances give vibrancy to fiestas across the country.
  • Historical Figure: Poet, Rubén Darío, father of Spanish-American literary movement, Modernism.

Ranked Must-See Destinations

Dest. 1

One of the must-see destinations in Nicaragua is Matagalpa, Nicaragua's "Selva Negra". The greenry brings tranquility to all those who visit.

Dest. 2

A cozy corner and very home-like street to visit is San Juan del Sur, Nicaragua.

Dest. 3

High on this list is also Masaya, Nicaragua. One can find in any restaurant in Masaya, the flavorful and traditional "Sopa de Mondongo".

Dest. 4

Before the day is over, one must visit Villa Carlos Fonseca - Playa Masachapa. The black sands come from the active volcanos that surround it.

Dest. 5

To close out your vacation with a beautiful sunset, Plata Masachapa does not disappoint.
